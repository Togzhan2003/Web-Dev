Best Cafes in Myeongdong

Publication date: 15/01/2023
  1. Myeongdong Kyoja

    2. The restaurant is known for its kalguksu, mandu, pibim kuksu and konguksu. Many people come here every day, mainly because all the food is prepared on the spot. The decor and service are simple, but the portions are significant, and the prices are reasonable.

  2. Hadongkwan

    3. A traditional Korean restaurant, cozy and not too fancy. There is another branch in Garosu gil, Gangnam. Order Korean cold noodles called Nanmen along with kalbi. The Korean family entertainment show "The Return of Superman" told about this restaurant.

  3. Yugan Mendon

    4. Since 1981, Yoogane has become one of the most popular brands of gobi chicken in South Korea. Galbi is the name of a wide range of Korean grilled dishes made from meat marinated in ganjang sauce (soy sauce). Their unique feature is the side dishes that you can add to the Galbi chicken to cook them together in a frying pan.

The Most Popular Korean Dramas On Netflix

Publication date: 06/01/2023
  1. Crash Landing on You

    2. This drama is about the tender love between a rich heiress who made an emergency landing in North Korea due to an accident while paragliding. She meets a North Korean officer who falls in love while trying to hide and protect her. A drama that was popular in South Korea with an audience rating of 21.7%. Because he is supported by veteran actors who demonstrate perfect North Korean dialects, which, as you know, differ from the dialects of South Korea. Actor Hyun Bin received a special instruction from a North Korean language teacher a few months before filming. As a result, he played the perfect North Korean officer. The special situation in South Korea and the real reconstruction of North Korea will be more special for people all over the world.

  2. Beauty Inside

    3. Charming and inspiring drama that tells the story of Han Se Kyo, played by Seo Hyun Jin, who wakes up every day in a different body known only to those closest to her, and Seo Do Jae, played by Lee Min Ki, a successful but cold—blooded CEO suffering from prosopagnosia, a condition, because of which he can't recognize faces. The show is a unique combination of romance, comedy and fantasy, which makes it a pleasant viewing experience. Seo Hyun Jin and Lee Min Ki show exciting performances that are both sincere and intimate as they overcome the difficulties of self-acceptance and identity. The supporting actors also do an excellent job of reviving the story. "Beauty Inside" is a touching and original look at the theme of beauty, self—acceptance and love. A must-see for those who love a unique romantic comedy.

  3. Kill me, heal me.

    4. A popular Korean drama that has everything you need. The series, based on a popular webtoon, tells a strong and touching story about friendship, family and the pursuit of success. Led by an exciting performance by actor Park Seo Joon, the show's strong cast delivers great performances and has chemistry that makes on-screen interactions even more believable. Thanks to the perfect balance of genres, including comedy, drama and melodrama, the series also touches on serious issues such as class, discrimination and injustice, but that's not all. There are also emotional moments in it. A well-written, well-acted and well-acted series that will keep you on your toes until the very end.
