Pancakes

  • Time: 30 min
  • Rating: 4.7/5
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 0.5litr
Eggs 3 pcs
Vegetable oil 1 tablespoon
Flour 250 g

Step 1: Mix eggs, salt, sugar and stir with a mixer. Enter the flour and pour in the milk. Beat the pancake batter to achieve a homogeneous consistency.

Step 2: Preheat the pan and grease with oil.

Step 3: Pour a small portion of dough into the center of the pan. Immediately rotate the pan in a circle so that the dough is evenly distributed over the entire surface.
